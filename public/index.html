Live Z-Axis (g)
Real-time vertical vibration
Processing Pipeline
  1. MPU9250 hardware sensor registers physical vertical G-force.
  2. ESP8266 micro-controller transmits raw integer packet via WebSocket.
  3. Frontend instantly receives, parses, and displays the most recent packet.
Raw Acceleration
  1. Direct visualization of raw WebSocket data packets.
  2. Plots Z-Axis G-force against the arrival timestamp.
  3. Oldest data points are popped off the array to create a sliding oscilloscope effect.
Vibration Delta (Jerk)
  1. Calculates the mathematical derivative of the raw acceleration.
  2. Subtracts the previous Z-axis reading from the current reading.
  3. Isolates and highlights sudden, sharp physical shocks while ignoring slow tilt.
Natural Frequency Analysis (FFT)
  1. Takes the accumulated time-domain data from the active session.
  2. Applies standard FFT to map the data from Time vs. Acceleration to Frequency vs. Amplitude.
  3. Visually exposes the structural resonance (tallest peak) and harmonic signatures.
